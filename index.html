About Us

Welcome to PSR Farm House

PSR Farm House is a peaceful and well-maintained garden farmhouse, ideal for family gatherings, parties, and small celebrations. Located in the serene countryside of Telangana, our venue offers a perfect escape from the city hustle.

Visitors appreciate the clean environment, pleasant atmosphere, and budget-friendly arrangements, making it a good choice for spending quality time with friends and family. Whether you're planning a birthday party, family reunion, or a relaxing day out, PSR Farm House provides the perfect setting.
